Cryptography Puzzle

Welcome to the puzzle!

To solve each puzzle, choose a cipher from the selector and enter a key in the text box.

Each puzzle has a different key/cipher combination

Press the hint button to get hints, with a maximum of 5

It is recommended that you start on puzzle 1 and progress to puzzle 5, but it is possible to do the puzzles independently

ROT13 does not require any key, caesar requires a number, vigenere requires a word, secret requires a number and a word in the format (number),(word).
